About Me

Hi, I'm Wenuja Dharmapala. I am an aspiring Technology & ICT student with passion for exploring the intersection of Network Technology, hardware engineering, programming and modern frontend design.

I completed my secondary education at Rahula College, Matara. I’ll soon begin my BICT degree at the University of Sri Jayewardenapura, where I plan to specialize in network systems, embedded technologies, and modern web development.

My interests include Networking, Cyber-security, Web Design, and Data-driven Applications. I enjoy experimenting with Arduino, Python, and front-end frameworks like React.

I believe technology should simplify life and empower creativity — that’s what drives every project I build.

Outside of Coding, I love exploring Nature, EDM Music Culture, Buddhist philosophy and learning how mindfulness can shape better problem-solving.
